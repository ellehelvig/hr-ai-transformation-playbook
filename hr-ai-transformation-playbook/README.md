## Why this exists

Most HR AI guidance falls into one of two traps: too theoretical to act on, or too vendor-specific to generalize. This playbook is neither. It's a working toolkit — prompts that run, notebooks that execute, governance checklists that hold up to legal review, and a use case library grounded in measurable business outcomes.

It is maintained by practitioners, for practitioners.

## Design principles

**Outcome-first.** Every use case, prompt, and model in this playbook is anchored to a measurable HR or business outcome. No technology for its own sake.

**Governance built in.** Responsible AI considerations are embedded in every section, not bolted on at the end.

**HRIS-agnostic.** Nothing here assumes Workday, SAP, or ServiceNow. Integration patterns are documented as adapters you wire to your own stack.

**Humans in the loop.** This playbook treats AI as a force multiplier for HR professionals, not a replacement. Every automated workflow has a defined escalation path to a human.
